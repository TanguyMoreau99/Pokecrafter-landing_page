Features

Pokecrafter is a vibrant social network that allows users to share their artistic creations inspired by the beloved Pokémon universe. Our platform is designed to encourage creativity and foster connections among artists of all skill levels. Join us to explore an array of exciting features!

Account Creation

Easily sign up and create your account to access a personalized profile, connect with fellow artists, and showcase your unique artwork.

Login

Log in to your account to continue sharing, discovering, and interacting with amazing artworks from the community.

Profile

On your profile, you can add a description, see who you arre following and who is following you. You can also Change your profile picture and access to the artworks you posted.

Main Feed

After you log in, you can access to the main feed. You can like, comment or follow people. You can also filter artworks by categories such as AI, 3D, Handcraft and more!

Instant Messaging

Talk with other Pokecrafter user with the messaging feature! Add them to your contact list before talking to them!

Contests

Participate to Contests to win a trophy ! If it's an official Contest, win a black trophy. If it's a user Contest submission, win a gold, silver a bronze trophy!

Post your own Artwork!

Upload your file, add a description, choose a category and get ready to get liked by other Pokecrafter users!

About Pokecrafter

Pokecrafter was born from a passion for art and community. As an artist and developer, I wanted to create a platform where people could share their creativity inspired by the enchanting world of Pokémon. The journey began a few months ago when I realized the need for a community-driven space where artists of all skill levels could connect and inspire one another. This project is a part of my portfolio for Holberton School, showcasing a full-stack application with real-time features and interactive elements.

As a lifelong Pokémon fan, I have always been captivated by the vibrant colors and imaginative worlds they create. Clément, with his deep love for art honed through his studies at the Beaux-Arts, shares this vision. Together, we pour our hearts into this project, hoping to create a warm and welcoming space for artists everywhere. It’s more than just a project; it’s a dream we cherish deeply.
